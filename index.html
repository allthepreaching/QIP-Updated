About

Quality Industrial Products was founded in 2002 by Fred Betti. Fred has over 30 years of experience in the fastener and industrial supply field. He developed a very successful business and his clients started requesting more than just nuts and bolts. This lead to the formation of Quality Industrial Products, a complete full line industrial supply distributor with the capability to service all clients' needs throughout the United States. Our company is known for its exceptional customer service, innovative programs and competitive pricing. .
